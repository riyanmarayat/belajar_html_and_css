Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In this book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as Ultra-low-temperature poaching.

Next month, wre will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before November 30.
